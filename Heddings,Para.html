Computers

A computer is an electronic device that processes raw data into information, serving as an essential tool in modern life for efficiency, communication, and education. It operates using components like a CPU, memory, and input/output devices to perform tasks in various fields such as healthcare, business, and entertainment.

Computers have become essential in almost every field, including education, banking, healthcare, and entertainment. They are utilized for various tasks such as browsing the internet, sending emails, playing games, and designing complex projects. With increasing technological advancements, computers are becoming more compact, user-friendly, and powerful, making daily tasks faster and more manageable.
